Governor’s Project

Our District Governor, Gabriella Slootsky, has announced her District Wide Project! I am proud to present, Soles4Souls. Souls4Soles is a nonprofit organization that focuses on collecting unwanted or discarded clothes and providing them to people in need. With over 70 million pairs of shoes doanted, Soles4Souls has provided millions with new clothes and prevented the production of waste that would have resulted with the shoes being discarded. I genuinely, wholeheartly recommend and encourage you to donate at least one of your older pair of shoes that don’t fit you anymore. Save waste, make smiles.

District Project

I also am proud to announce this service year’s District Project, Pencils of Promise! Pencils of Promise works internationally to build schools for communities that need it. Pencils for Promise has a profound impact on people all around the worl, due to the very nature of its goal.. The opportunity for education is a amenity that people in developed countries take for granted. Education can provide the chance for families to create new beginnings and creep out of poverty. As they say, knowledge is power, and in a growing service based economy, it is ever more important an essential to get an education to survive.
